## Key Value Propositions

### The Problem (Traditional Roofing)
- **GI Sheets**: Rust within few years, trap heat, deafening rain noise, require repainting every 4 years
- **Fiber Cement**: ~12 year lifespan, heavy, regular repairs needed
- **Clay Tiles**: Heavy, fragile, break easily
- **Color-Coated Steel**: Paint fades, corrosion from salt/moisture exposure

### The N88E Build Solution
- **Material**: ASA-coated uPVC
- **Lifespan**: 30+ years (3x longer than alternatives)
- **Temperature**: 5-7°C cooler interiors
- **Noise**: Silent during rain (sound absorbing)
- **Maintenance**: Zero (no repainting, no rust, no repairs)
- **Weight**: Lightweight (easy transport and installation)
- **Cost**: Affordable with superior long-term value
